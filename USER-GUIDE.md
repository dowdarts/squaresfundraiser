## ğŸš€ Getting Started (For Organizers)

### Step 1: Create Your Free Account

1. Visit **https://squarefund.aadsdarts.com**
2. Click **"Start My Own Square Fund Board Today"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. You're in! Time to create your first fundraiser

### Step 2: Create Your Fundraiser

1. Click **"Create New Fundraiser"** in your dashboard
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: Give your fundraiser a catchy name (e.g., "Hawks Hockey Team Trip 2026")
   - **Description**: Explain what you're raising money for
   - **Square Price**: Set your price per square (e.g., $5, $10, $20)
   - **E-Transfer Email**: Where supporters should send payments
3. **Upload a Banner** (optional): Add a team logo or event photo to make your board stand out
4. Click **"Create Fundraiser"**
5. Your board is now live! ğŸ‰

### Step 3: Share Your Board

1. Copy your unique fundraiser link (e.g., `squarefund.aadsdarts.com/squares.html?id=your-id`)
2. Share it everywhere:
   - Text message to team parents
   - Post on social media
   - Email to your contact list
   - Share in group chats
   - Post to your organization's website

**Pro Tip**: Your board also appears in the "Active Fundraisers" lobby on the homepage, so people can discover it even without your direct link!

### Step 4: Monitor & Approve Payments

**Real-Time Dashboard**: Your admin dashboard shows:
- **Sold**: Confirmed paid squares
- **Pending**: Awaiting your approval
- **Available**: Open squares
- **Total Raised**: Running total

**When Someone Purchases Squares**:

1. **Buyer selects squares** on your public board
2. **You get notified** via the email you provided
3. **Buyer sends e-Transfer** to your email
4. **You receive the e-Transfer** in your bank account
5. **You approve the payment** in your dashboard under the "Payments" tab
6. **Buyer gets confirmation email** - their squares are locked in!
7. **Board updates** automatically in real-time

**Important**: Always verify you received the e-Transfer before approving payments in the dashboard!

### Step 5: Run the Lottery & Pick a Winner

When all squares are sold (or you're ready to draw):

1. Go to your dashboard and select your fundraiser
2. Click the **"Draw Winner"** tab
3. Click **"Run Lottery"**
4. Watch the random selection animation (20 spins for drama!)
5. **Winner is displayed** on your board and dashboard
6. Announce the winner to all participants!

**The randomizer is 100% fair** - every sold square has an equal chance of winning.

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### For Organizers:

**Q: How much does SquareFund cost?**  
A: **Zero.** No platform fees, no transaction fees, no hidden costs. 100% of the money goes to you.

**Q: How do I get paid?**  
A: Supporters send money directly to your e-Transfer email. It goes straight to your bank account - we never touch your money.

**Q: Can I create multiple fundraisers?**  
A: Yes! Create as many as you need. Manage them all from one dashboard.

**Q: What if someone doesn't pay after selecting squares?**  
A: Squares are only held for 2 minutes. If payment isn't submitted, they automatically become available again.

**Q: Can I edit my fundraiser after creating it?**  
A: Yes! Go to your dashboard, select the fundraiser, and use the Settings tab to update details.

**Q: How do I deactivate a fundraiser?**  
A: In Settings, toggle "Fundraiser Status" to Inactive. The board will no longer accept new purchases.

**Q: What happens if I lose internet during the lottery?**  
A: The winner is stored in the database permanently. Refresh your page to see the result.

### For Supporters:

**Q: Do I need to create an account to buy squares?**  
A: **No!** That's the beauty of SquareFund - just click, pay, done.

**Q: How long are my squares reserved?**  
A: 2 minutes after you click "Proceed to Payment." If you need more time, just select them again.

**Q: What if I pick the same squares as someone else?**  
A: Impossible! The reservation system prevents double-booking in real-time.

**Q: When will I know if I won?**  
A: The organizer will run the lottery when ready and announce the winner. The winner also appears on the public board.

**Q: Can I buy squares for someone else as a gift?**  
A: Yes! Just enter their email address when purchasing.

**Q: What if I sent payment but it wasn't approved?**  
A: Contact the organizer directly. They may not have seen your e-Transfer yet.
